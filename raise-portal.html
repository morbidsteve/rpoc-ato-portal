⚠ Actions That Require a Human

  • ✍
    Fill in placeholders — Every document has _[PLACEHOLDER]_ fields for org name, ISSM, dates. Search and replace across all docs.
  • 💻
    Register in eMASS — CAC required. Use the eMASS Guide (below) — all fields are pre-filled, just copy-paste into eMASS.
  • 💻
    Register in DITPR + PPSM — System tracking + ports/protocols registration. Data is in the Authorization Boundary doc.
  • 📝
    Sign documents — CI/CD Certification (TA), SLAs (App Owners), Rules of Behavior (each user), ATO letter (AO).
  • 👥
    Engage SCA — Independent assessor must evaluate controls and produce the SAR. Allow 8-12 weeks.
  • 🎯
    Brief the AO — Present the package (SSP + SAR + POA&M). AO makes the risk-based ATO decision.
